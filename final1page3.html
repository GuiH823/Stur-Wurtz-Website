Star Wars Battlefront 2


Star Wars Battlefront 2 is an upcoming first/third-person

shooter and is the sequel to the revival of the series. The

previous game (Star Wars Battlefront) was released in 2015,

and the new one will release Friday, November 17, 2017.

Unlike its predecessor, Battlefront 2 will include more maps,

heroes, singlepayer games, and a campaign. Battlefront 2

has recently become popular to many due to the controversy

between a "pay-to-win" look to the game during the beta in

October and the hype from the multiple trailers. You can see

both the trailers and the "pay to win" system right here.
